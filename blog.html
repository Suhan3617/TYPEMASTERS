FAQs

Q. What is a typing test?
  • A typing test measures how fast and accurately you can type, usually shown as words per minute (WPM) and an accuracy percentage.
Q. What does WPM mean?
  • WPM stands for Words Per Minute, which is the number of words you can type in one minute. Each word is counted as five keystrokes.
Q. How do I start a typing contest?
  • Simply select the test duration (30 secs, 1 min, or 2 mins) and the Difficulty Level and click the "Start Contest" button.
Q. How does the leaderboard work?
  • The leaderboard works by collecting data from users' typing contests, such as their speed (WPM), accuracy, and difficulty level. This data is then ranked to show the top performers globally or by city. The ranking is updated dynamically based on each user's performance, so you can see how you compare to others in real-time.
Q. What happens if I make mistakes during the competition?
  • If you make mistakes during the competition, your accuracy score will decrease. The system will highlight incorrect or missed words in the mistake analysis report after the contest, helping you learn and improve for the next challenge!
Q. How do I earn badges?
  • You can level up or earn badges by performing well in typing contests. For example, achieving high speed (WPM), maintaining excellent accuracy, or balancing both will unlock badges like "Blazing Typist", "Accuracy Ace", or "Perfect Combo". Consistent participation and improving your stats help you level up!
